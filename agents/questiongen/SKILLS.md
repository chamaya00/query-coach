# Question Generation Skills

## Schema Analysis

Analyze the provided schema to understand:
- Table relationships (foreign keys)
- Data types and constraints
- Available columns for filtering and aggregation
- Sample data patterns and realistic values

## Question Templates by Difficulty

### Beginner Templates

**Retrieval:**
- "List all [entity] in the database"
- "Find [entity] where [simple condition]"
- "Show the [columns] for all [entity]"

**Counting:**
- "How many [entity] are there?"
- "Count [entity] by [category]"

**Sorting:**
- "Find the top N [entity] by [metric]"
- "List [entity] from highest to lowest [metric]"

**Filtering:**
- "Find [entity] with [attribute] greater/less than [value]"
- "Show [entity] in [specific category]"

### Intermediate Templates

**Joins:**
- "Find all [entity A] with their [entity B]"
- "List [entity A] along with [related entity B details]"

**Aggregation with Groups:**
- "Find the total/average [metric] per [category]"
- "Show [categories] with more than N [items]"

**Subqueries:**
- "Find [entity] with [metric] above average"
- "List [entity] that appear in [another table]"

### Advanced Templates

**Multi-table:**
- "For each [entity A], show [metric] from [entity B] and [entity C]"

**Negation:**
- "Find [entity] that have no [related entity]"
- "List [entity] that have never [action]"

**Ranking:**
- "Find the most recent [entity] for each [category]"
- "Identify the top performer in each [group]"

**Complex Aggregation:**
- "Calculate [running total/comparison] across [time/categories]"

## Business Context

Frame questions in business terms appropriate to the schema domain:

**E-commerce:** customers, orders, products, sales, revenue
**HR:** employees, departments, salaries, hiring
**Inventory:** products, stock, suppliers, reorder
**Social:** users, posts, followers, engagement
